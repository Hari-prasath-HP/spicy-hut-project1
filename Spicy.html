Craving delicious Indian and Fast-food favourites? Look no further than Spicy Hut Sanquhar located at 1 Main St, Sanquhar DG4 6BN! Our family-owned restaurant takes pride in using only the freshest, high-quality local ingredients to create flavourful and authentic dishes.

From our signature Chicken Tikka Masala simmered in a rich, creamy tomato sauce to our perfectly crispy Fish and Chips made with a light, golden batter, each menu item is prepared fresh with care by our experienced chefs. You'll find all your favourite Indian curries like hearty Kormas, aromatic Biryanis, and tandoori specialties straight from the clay oven. We also offer an array of mouthwatering Fast-food options including freshly baked Pizzas, juicy Burgers, flavourful Wraps, and sizzling Kebabs.

The best part? You can enjoy all these delicious eats from the comfort of your home or office. Simply order online through our website for quick delivery or collection at a time that suits you.
